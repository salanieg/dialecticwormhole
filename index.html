Ἀγεωμέτρητος μηδεὶς εἰσίτω.
No one should enter without knowledge of the geometry.

Entropy shifts order into chaos. Not because it is necessary, however, but because it is the most probable outcome. The pure destruction of geometry, whether physical or discursive, is a masculine act. To arrange what has been destroyed, to synthesise it, is the legacy of Gaía. To force the shattered geometry through a dialectical wormhole, and to reassemble it, that is the desideratum of this website.
